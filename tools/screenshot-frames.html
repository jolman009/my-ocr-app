Play Store Screenshot Frames

Frames are pre-loaded from screenshots/. Click any phone to swap in a different image.

How to use:
1. All five frames auto-load from tools/screenshots/ (Scan, Review, Export, Dark Mode, Dashboard).
2. To swap any pre-loaded screenshot, click the phone area to pick a new file.
3. Right-click each frame → Save image as... or use the export button below.
4. Upload the saved images to Google Play Console.

Recommended size: Play Store wants 1080x1920 px. These frames render at 40% scale for preview. The export button generates full-resolution images.

Marketing graphics: finished assets in tools/screenshots/marketing/ (Built for the Solo Pro, Scan Every Detail, etc.) are already Play Store-ready and don't need wrapping — upload them directly.
