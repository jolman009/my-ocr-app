Play Store Screenshot Frames

Click each phone to add a screenshot. Right-click a frame to save as image.

How to use:
1. Take screenshots from the app on your device or emulator
2. Click the phone area in each frame to load your screenshot
3. Right-click each frame → Save image as... or use the export button below
4. Upload the saved images to Google Play Console

Recommended size: Play Store wants 1080x1920 px. These frames render at 40% scale for preview. The export button generates full-resolution images.
